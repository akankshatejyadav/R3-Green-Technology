    About Us

    We are a group of people consciously saving mother nature and have great thrust (not always, but mostly) ! Saving water with little efforts with limited money, we realised preserving fresh water is not a difficult path and we wanted to share this with the world !

    So after realizing the value of water and studying pattern of water usage, R-Cube Green Technology was born.

    Our Mission

    We aim to use our experience to share ways, gadgets and methods to preserve precious water.
